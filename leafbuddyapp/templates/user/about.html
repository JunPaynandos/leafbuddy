About LeafBuddy

LeafBuddy is an AI-powered tool built to help farmers identify plant diseases early, improve crop health, and increase productivity. We believe in empowering agriculture through technology—making disease detection accessible, accurate, and user-friendly for everyone, especially small-scale farmers.

Our Mission

To revolutionize agriculture by providing AI-driven solutions that help farmers detect leaf diseases quickly, accurately, and affordably—reducing crop loss and promoting sustainable farming practices.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every farmer, regardless of location or scale, can access cutting-edge plant health diagnostics and make data-driven decisions to boost yield and protect their crops.

Meet the Team

We’re a passionate team of developers, agronomists, and designers dedicated to building accessible and innovative tools for modern agriculture.
